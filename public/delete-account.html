Delete Account

Users can request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

To delete your account, please follow one of the methods below:

  • Open the app → Go to Settings → Delete Account
  • Or contact us at: support@yourapp.com

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User profile information
  • Uploaded content
  • Account credentials

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for legal or security purposes for up to 30 days.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 working days.
